Little Joy Returns

The return of Little Joy has thrilled indie music lovers around the world. Formed in 2007, the trio formed by Rodrigo Amarante, Fabrizio Moretti and Binki Shapiro left an indelible mark with their first and only eponymous album, released in 2008. Their unique style, a mix of indie rock, bossa nova and soft melodies, won over a diverse audience, creating a loyal fan base.

After more than a decade of silence, the announcement of their comeback has generated a wave of excitement and expectations. Rumors of a possible reunion began to circulate earlier this year when the band members were spotted together at various musical events. Finally, confirmation came through social media, where they shared their excitement to create music together again.
